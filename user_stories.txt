*** User Story ***
As an employee
I want to know my montly income
So I can keep track of my finance

Acceptance Criteria
*Print employee's name.
*Print gross salary before 30% tax.
*Print net pay.

*** User Story ***
As a quota sales person or owner
I want to know my bonus
So I can keep track of my finance

Acceptance Criteria
*Print my bonus.
*Net pay should include taxed bonus and monthly salary.

*** User Story ***
As a commission sales person
I want to know how much commision I got
So I can keep track of my finance

Acceptance Criteria
*Print my commision.
*Net pay should include taxed commision and monthly salary.
